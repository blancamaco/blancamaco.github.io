A little bit about myself üôã

I have always been a creative person interested in new technologies and design. Since I was a child, I have been painting, drawing, filming and creating lots of things, but I also have been really curious about the computer my father had at home, which I used to play videogames and draw with Paint.

After high-school, I decided to study Computer Engineering so I could understand technology better, while in my free time I developed my creativity working as a visual designer for a startup with some colleagues. It was then when I discovered the digital product design field, one that mixed my two major interests, design and technology. That encouraged me to learn more about it by reading books, taking online courses about UX/UI design and studying a Graphic Design Master after my Bachelor.

The following years I worked on another project as a Frontend Developer and Web Designer, taking care of the product from the interface to the code. Nowadays I keep learning everyday as a Product Designer in Geoblink. During those experiences, I discovered that I really enjoy working in motivated and dynamic teams to bring useful, enjoyable and easy to use products to life.
