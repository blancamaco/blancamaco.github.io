A little bit about myself :)

My name is Blanca Madorrán, I am a Web, Mobile and Graphic Designer focused on UX and UI. I am currently finishing a Master in Graphic Design at the Polytechnic University of Valencia, where I previously studied a Bachelor in Computer Engineering. To complement my studies I also did several online courses to improve my UX/UI skills.

I have always been a creative person interested in technology and design. Since I was a child, I have been painting, drawing and creating lots of things, but I also have been really curious about the computer and the technological gadgets I had at home, developing an interest in technology. During my Computer Engineering studies I found a way to mix my two major interest by designing interfaces and user experiences for digital devices. To learn and gain experience in it, I took part on several startups outside the classroom as a Graphic Designer and Frontend developer for both mobile and web devices, together with multidisciplinary teams. It was during those experiences when I discovered that I really enjoy working in motivated and dynamic teams to bring a new product to life.

In order to experience working in an international environment, I also decided to do a Computer Engineering semester abroad in Germany, where learnt how to effectively communicate with people of different nationalities and cultures. After that, to improve on my design and visual communication skills, I decided to study a Master in Graphic Design.

Throughout all these experiences I have developed a powerful combination of skills, being able to develop digital products from the conceptual design and analysis part to de coding part, allowing me to effectively communicate with the different parts of the team and identify their needs and limitations. You can find my detiled experience down below, in my CV or my LinkedIn.
