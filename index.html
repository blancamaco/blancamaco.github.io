When I'm not working, you can catch me:

    📸 Taking photos of nature and architecture.

    🎶 Going to concerts.

    👾 Playing video games

    🖼️ Spending hours lost in museums.

    📖 Reading science fiction and design books.

    🗺 Travelling (if there's no pandemic).

Since I was a child, I have been painting, drawing, filming and creating all kinds of things. When my father brought a computer to home, I discovered endless ways it could help me to create those things and many more.

After high-school, I decided to study Computer Engineering so I could understand technology better, while in my free time I exploited my creativity working as a visual designer on side projects and startups. It was then when I discovered the UX/UI field, a mixture of my two major interests, design and technology, by studing how people interact with technological products. That encouraged me to study a Graphic Design Master and take some online UX/UI courses after my Bachelor to gain more knowledge about the field.

Nowadays I keep learning everyday and creating better digital products as a Product Designer in Geoblink.

You can find my detailed experience down below, in my CV or my LinkedIn.
