Working as a Product Designer in Geoblink I'm learning a lot about data representation and information architecture, as well as improving my research skills and conducting tests with users.

What's Geoblink?

Geoblink is a SaaS-based Location Intelligence solution that helps professionals from the retail, real estate, and FMCG industries make informed decisions about their business strategies. It combines traditional and non-traditional advanced analytics techniques over big and small data, together with a rich map-based UI to display multiple types of statistics in a way that is simple to use and easy to understand.

What do I do?

As part of the Product team, I deal with data representation, data consumption and information architecture challenges every day, applying user-centred design to solve them in order to provide the best experience to our users. I'm also constantly working on improving the user experience of the application in general, participating in research initiatives, designing new features and presenting design decisions to the team.

We regularly collect users feedback from the Customer Success team to fix usability problems, iterate over previous designs, discover new user pain points or start research initiatives for possible new functionalities.

Our application's visual design is based on a design system that we're always updating and maintaining to give the application a consistent style and communicate efficiently with the development team. Take a look to the Geoblink Design System.
